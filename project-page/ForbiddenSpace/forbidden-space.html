01 — STORY

Trapped in a Cosmic Loop

In a remote corner of the universe lies a mysterious place known as "The Forbidden Space." It is the domain of a four-dimensional extraterrestrial being capable of manipulating time itself.

Players take control of a spacecraft named J4-RW0 — "Jarwo" — who accidentally enters The Forbidden Space. Trapped within this strange realm, Jarwo becomes nothing more than a plaything for the powerful entity, which endlessly resets and repeats time, forcing him to relive the same events over and over again.

03 — MECHANICS

How It Works

Time-Loop Progression

Death is not the end. Every failure resets the timeline — restart the journey while learning enemy patterns and improving survival strategies.

Twin-Stick Sci-Fi Combat

Fast-paced space combat focused on movement, positioning, and aiming precision. Mouse-aimed, WASD-driven — arcade meets tactical.

Boss Battles

Optional boss encounters with unique combat phases. Currently in development — accessible from the main menu for early testing.

Planet Diversity

Each planet introduces different enemy species with unique attack patterns — aggressive rammers, projectile-based weapon systems, and dimensional threats.
