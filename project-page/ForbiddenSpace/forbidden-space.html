Project 01 — 2024

Forbidden Space

Unity Engine 3D Puzzle Solo Dev

A dark atmospheric puzzle experience that bends the rules of space and gravity. Navigate through impossible geometry in zero-G environments.

Overview

Beyond the
Known Grid

Forbidden Space challenges the player to rethink what they understand about physical space. Built with custom physics constraints and shader-driven environments, every room is a puzzle of perception — where walls become floors and gravity is just a suggestion.

Concept

Space Bending Physics

Custom-built gravity manipulation using Unity's Rigidbody system. Each zone defines its own gravitational vector, creating seamless transitions between floor, wall, and ceiling traversal.

Visual

Dark Atmospheric Shaders

GLSL-driven post-processing stack with custom depth fog, chromatic aberration on gravity shifts, and a procedural star field that responds to player orientation.

Audio

Reactive Soundscape

Ambient audio layers that shift in real-time based on the current gravity state. Low-frequency drones fade in as the player enters zero-G zones, heightening the sense of disorientation.
